# Night Shift Wizard: Arcane Overdrive 🧙‍♂️✨


**Night Shift Wizard: Arcane Overdrive** is a fast-paced, HTML5 pixel-art survival game. Dodge cursed hazards, catch celestial bodies, and hoard Arcane Shards to purchase permanent upgrades and relics from the Wizard's Shop. Featuring a built-in global leaderboard, meta-progression, and secret synergy events, it's designed for quick, highly replayable runs.


## 🌟 Key Features


* **Arcade Survival:** Catch Stars and Comets to build your score and combo multiplier, while dodging Skulls, Phantoms, Bats, and Specters.
* **Roguelite Meta-Progression:** Collect persistent Arcane Shards (♦) during runs to spend in the Wizard's Shop.
* **The Wizard's Shop:** Upgrade your Shield Potions, Hermes Boots, Magnet Jewels, and Laser Blasts using an exponential cost-scaling system.
* **Unlockable Wardrobe:** Save up to unlock and equip alternate pixel-art skins, including the Pyromancer and Necromancer.
* **Strategic Relics:** Modify your playstyle before a run. Equip the "Glass Cannon" for massive score multipliers at the cost of your shields, or "Lead Boots" to crush enemies while moving sluggishly.
* **Synergy Events:** Collect specific power-ups in sequential order (Potion -> Star -> Magnet) to trigger a screen-wiping Arcane Burst.
* **Boss Encounters:** Survive long enough to face off against animated pixel-art bosses (Dragon, Eye, Slime, Lich). Test your mettle in the dedicated **Boss Rush** mode.
* **Global Leaderboards:** Powered by Supabase, compete against other wizards worldwide for the top 10 high scores.


## 🎮 How to Play


### Controls
* **Mouse/Touch:** Drag or swipe to move the Wizard left and right.
* **Keyboard:** Use the `Left Arrow` / `Right Arrow` (or `A` / `D`) keys to move.
* **Pause:** Press `Esc` or `P`, or click the pause button in the top right.


### Mechanics
* **Catch (Good):** Stars (White), Comets (Gold), Arcane Shards (Purple).
* **Dodge (Bad):** Skulls (Red), Phantoms (Purple), Specters (White tracking), Bats (Grey splitting).
* **Power-ups:**
    * 🧪 **Potion:** Grants a hit-absorbing shield.
    * 👟 **Boots:** Grants massive speed and the ability to ram enemies.
    * 💎 **Jewel:** Magnetizes loot toward the player.
    * 💥 **Blast:** Fires a vertical laser that destroys hazards for bonus points.
